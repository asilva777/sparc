WELCOME!
Disasters can strike at any time, and it's crucial for SMEs to be prepared.
The SME Disaster Preparedness and Resilience Check (SPARC) uses the CASE Equation:
Resilience = (Coping + Adaptability) - (Sensitivity + Exposure)

By proceeding, you consent to the collection of your personal information solely for resilience evaluation. All data is confidential.
